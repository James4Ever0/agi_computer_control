usage (python version):

install vimgolf: pip install vimgolf

play an online game: vimgolf put <challenge-number>

scraping vimgolf.com

we can scrape all challenges without login, and the least favorable solution.

we have to sign in and submit higher rank solutions to get lower rank ones.

visit a specific challenge, see definition and lowest rank solution:

https://www.vimgolf.com/challenges/9v0067401f2500000000061b

iterate challenges by page number, currently 13 pages total

https://www.vimgolf.com/?page=2

you can iterate with commandline too: vimgolf list [PAGE]

to prevent dns lookup error during scraping, just write the resolved ip of vimgolf.com to /etc/hosts
